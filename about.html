Greetings!

I am a recently graduated Architectural designer, in the process of transitioning to a career in UX research and design. The idea that User Experience is a perfect blend of design, technology, human behaviour, and psychology, is something that intrigues me a lot. I believe that design should not only be visual but should solve real-world problems and enhance human-product interaction.

User research, designing user-centric spaces while keeping in mind client goals, carrying out multiple design iterations, creating visually appealing graphics, and making design presentations has been a crucial part of my 5 years of architectural education and practice. I strongly believe I can use these skills and experience acquired in the past few years, to transcend in the field of UX design.


Things that make me smile- Music, books, photography, travelling, and lots of food! :)
